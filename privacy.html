Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

Dorak collects information you provide directly to us when you create an account, update your profile, or book a service. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and appointment history.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including processing your bookings and communicating with you about appointments.

3. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with the specific professionals you book with so they can fulfill your service. We implement strict security measures to protect your information.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information within the Dorak app settings at any time.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@dorak.dev
